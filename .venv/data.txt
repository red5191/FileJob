London London is the capital of Great Britain, its political, economic and commercial centre.

It is one of the largest cities in the world and the largest city in Europe.

Its pop­ulation is about 8 million. London is situated on the river Thames.

The city is very old. It has more than 20 centuries old history.

Tra­ditionally it is divided into several parts, the City, West­minster, the West End and the East, End.
